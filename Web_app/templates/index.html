This project is trained from tweets acquired by twitter firehose. By using n-gram and stupid backoff model, I'm able to provide you with preditions of your next word as well as a emoji you might want to use.

In order to provide emoji predictions, I use a unsupervised learning of Word2Vec model to featurize the word and find emoji that has high similarity to the predicted word by stupid backoff n_gram model.
